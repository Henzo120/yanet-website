    SUSTAINABILITY

    LHC firmly believes in carrying out its operations in a societally and environmentally sustainable manner. As a company whose mission is providing integrated healthcare solutions for the wellbeing of humanity, delivering on its commitment to societal and environmental sustainability constitutes the core philosophy of its business.

    LHC endeavors to materialize this philosophy through a growing list of interventions, including:

    • Proactively responding to existing and evolving societal healthcare needs through the delivery of rare and highly demanded services and products.
    • Locally availing advanced healthcare services that often are the reasons citizens travel lengthy distances as far as overseas for.
    • Exercising economy-of-scale and value chain optimization through integration of services and products to enable the delivery of services and products at affordable fees.
    • Upholding partnership business model and taking the lead to engage actors in the healthcare value chain to help render services and products to the wider national and regional public at affordable fees.
    • Ensuring healthcare quality through the implementation of national and global operational quality standards as well as fostering the training of competent healthcare workforce.
    • Creating job opportunities for a growing number of citizens.
    • Providing technical, financial, and material assistance for the public good during regular times and crises such as the COVID pandemic.
    • Delivering services free of charge and at subsidized rates for economically disadvantaged segments of communities.
    • Demonstrating the utmost compliance to regulatory and statutory standards.

    The recognitions LHC received from across all levels of regional and national authorities is attest to the Company’s effort towards sustainability.
