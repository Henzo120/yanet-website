Our History

LIYANA Healthcare Plc. (LHC): is an integrated healthcare Solutions Company located in Hawassa, Ethiopia, approximately 275 km south of Addis Ababa. The company's journey began with the establishment of Unity Higher Clinic in July 2005 in Dilla town, a successful internal medicine facility founded by Dr. Girma Ababi and his colleagues. Within a year, the clinic expanded by opening Selam Higher Clinic in the same town. In 2012, Dr. Girma moved to Hawassa city and established Yanet Internal Medicine Higher Clinic (YIMHC), which quickly grew into a prominent medical service provider. By November 2013, YIMHC had launched Yanet Drugs and Medical Supplies Wholesale (YD&MSWS), becoming a major distributor of medical supplies in Southern Ethiopia. In December 2016, the clinic was upgraded to Yanet Internal Medicine Specialized Center (YIMSC), offering an array of specialized internal medicine services with advanced medical technologies and highly qualified specialists. In July 2018, LIYANA Healthcare Plc. was officially formed, incorporating Yanet Trauma and Surgery Specialty Center as its core business entity, and later adding Yanet Internal Medicine Specialty Center, Yanet Drugs and Medical Supplies Wholesale, and Yanet Drugs and Medical Supplies Import in July 2019. Today, LHC operates six active business lines and is on the verge of launching two additional facilities. The company has also initiated major projects, including Yanet Hospital in Addis Ababa and Yanet Cancer Center in Hawassa, aiming to enhance its healthcare services and extend its impact across the region.

Yante-Liyana College of Health science(YLCHC)

Yanet-Liyana College of Health Sciences, part of Liyana Healthcare PLC, is located in Hawassa and was established in 2021. The college offers two postgraduate and four undergraduate programs focused on health sciences, aiming to excel in teaching, research, and community engagement by 2027. It emphasizes competency-based training through theoretical and practical learning, upholding the motto "Quality Is Non-Negotiable." The institution provides unique health-specialized programs, scholarships, internships at Yanet clinical facilities, discounted medical care for students, and potential job placements within Liyana Healthcare PLC.The college has recently expanded its infrastructure and academic offerings, including leasing a G+3 building and adding three new postgraduate programs. Accredited by Hawassa University as a Continuous Professional Development (CPD) provider, it offers short-term training certified by the Ministry of Health. Known for its academic freedom, public trust, and secure management system, Yanet-Liyana College maintains high academic standards, a strong pass rate, and successful student placements. The college is managed by a team of deans, coordinators, and committees, representing excellence in healthcare education.

Yanet Drug and Medical Equipment Import & Wholesale

Owing to the success of YDMSWS in the pharmaceuticals distribution sector, and the solid experience acquired in the medical supplies chain management, and the ever increasing demand for healthcare products, LIYANA Healthcare has recently (June 2019) launched Yanet Drugs and Medical supplies Import Division (YDMSI). The division import Ethiopian Food and Drugs Administration (FDA) approved high quality drugs and medical supplies and distributes to medical supplies wholesalers. The division has acquired appropriate premise, work permission and license for its operation. It is the first of its type in southern and central Ethiopia.

Yanet Intenal Medicine & Surgical Center(YIMSC)

located in Addis Ababa, is a reputable healthcare facility that has been integral to the community since it first received a COVID-19 treatment license on August 29, 2013. The center played a crucial role during the global pandemic by treating COVID-19 patients and later obtained an internal medicine service qualification license on January 30, 2013. Since then, it has consistently provided high-quality medical services, focusing on patient care and community health needs. The center is staffed by a dedicated team of internal medicine specialists and support personnel, ensuring high standards of care for patients. Known for its clean and comfortable environment, Yanete has excelled in treating chronic conditions and providing kidney treatments. With a commitment to affordability, the facility competes effectively with local healthcare providers by offering quality services. Currently employing 105 permanent and temporary staff, Yanete collaborates with nearby government institutions to provide discounted diagnostic services and continuous training for healthcare professionals, fulfilling its social responsibilities while preparing to upgrade to a general hospital and initiate kidney transplantation in the private sector.

Yante Primary Hospital(YPH)

Yanet Primary Hospital (YPH) is a key member of the LIYANA Healthcare family, dedicated to providing comprehensive medical care to the community. As a sister company within this esteemed healthcare network, YPH prides itself on offering a wide range of essential health services, ensuring that patients receive top-quality care. The hospital is particularly noted for its specialized services, including dialysis and chemotherapy, as well as newly introduced services like Maternal and Child Health (MCH) and Neonatal Intensive Care Unit (NICU) services. YPH boasts a variety of departments designed to cater to diverse medical needs. These include the Outpatient Department, Inpatient Department, and Emergency Department, which form the core of its healthcare delivery. The hospital also features a dedicated MNCH (Maternal, Neonatal, and Child Health) Department, a Dialysis Department, and a Chemotherapy Department. Additionally, YPH is equipped with a Radiology Unit and an Operating Room (OR) Unit, ensuring comprehensive diagnostic and surgical services are available to its patients.

LHC-RCT, the research and development

Liyana Healthcare, provides health service and management consultancy services for both in-house and external customers. The division supports organizational and business development initiatives and offers consultancy in strategic decision-making, health research, service management, baseline surveys, feasibility studies, project management, evaluations, business strategy, financial management, corporate governance, HR management, supply chain management, and short-term management training. LHC-RCT is well-equipped with the necessary premises and professionals.

Yanet Trauma and Surgical Specialized Center (YTSSC)

a division of LIYANA Healthcare, provides specialized trauma management and surgical care by highly qualified orthopedists and surgeons. YTSSC offers high-quality, compassionate care on both an outpatient and inpatient basis through its Trauma Management and Surgery Departments. The Trauma Management Department handles acute care and trauma surgeries, including orthopedic, plastic, neurosurgery, and more. The surgical department offers various surgical services, including emergencies and intensive care. YTSSC operates 24/7 with advanced diagnostic and treatment facilities, and has the capacity to cater to up to 250 clients daily.

Yanet Internal Medicine Specialized Center (YIMSC)

a division of LIYANA Healthcare, specializes in advanced internal medicine clinical services. YIMSC offers 24/7 specialty services including Neurology, Gastroenterology, ENT, Dermatology, Women's health, Cancer treatment, Hemodialysis, and Psychiatry. They are equipped with state-of-the-art diagnostic and therapeutic technologies like MRI, CT-Scan, and advanced clinical laboratories. Recently, YIMSC was chosen by Ethiopia's Federal Ministry of Health to partner with GAMCA for medical examinations for Ethiopian expatriates heading to the Gulf countries.

Yali Detergent & Cosmotic Manufacturing(YDMSWS)

a branch of LIYANA Healthcare, specializes in the wholesale distribution of drugs and medical supplies. Established in November 2013, YDMSWS quickly became a major distributor in central and southern Ethiopia. The division ensures access to essential drugs and medical supplies through an efficient supply chain management system, supported by dependable suppliers and numerous loyal customers, including pharmacies and drug stores throughout the region

Liyana Digital Pharmaceuticals and Medical Supplies Import

This unit was established to address the current and future need of healthcare products (drugs, medical equipment, and supplies) of the company, its sister companies, and the country as a whole. The company has registered a long list of products from reputable manufacturers from around the world as their first and sole agent, and many more are in the process of being registered. This business unit is designed to help the company grow financially and address the dire shortage of specific drugs and medical equipment that would have a significant impact on healthcare delivery in Ethiopia.

Dream-Liyana Healthcare PLC (Addis Ababa)

This company was established in collaboration with five seasoned specialist and subspecialist orthopedic,neuro and spine surgeons with the goal of addressing the significant gap in high-end orthopedic, trauma, and neurosurgery care. The senior physicians, in this unit, have rare subspecialties, a large pool of patients in Addis Ababa and the regions, as well as in Somaliland, Eritrea, and other neighboring countries. The acquisition of a premise (hospital facility) in Addis Ababa, Lideta sub city, near the Africa Union headquarters, has already been completed. Renovation and furnishing are already underway. Dream Orthopedic and Trauma Specialty Center is expected to be operational by the first week of August 2015.

Cheliyan Healthcare PLC (Addis Ababa)

CHC is a joint venture initiative between LDHCS (with a larger share) and Chechella Medical Services PLC, which is under formation in Addis Ababa with a capital of 200 million ETB. This initiative is expected to address the challenge of hundreds of patients who daily travel around the city in search of diagnostic, therapeutic, and pharmacy services that are not available at public hospitals. This multispecialty tertiary care center will provide high-end medical, surgical, and trauma services, as well as state-of-the-art and latest imaging and laboratory services. The Plc. owns an 8-story facility, which is currently undergoing renovation. The procurement of diagnostic and therapeutic equipment is underway. The facility is expected to be operational by the end of July 2015 E.C.

MAAL Healthcare PLC (Addis Ababa)

This company was established in collaboration with three renowned and experienced obstetrician-gynecologists and surgeons who are focusing on significant unmet need for high-end obstetric, gynecological, pediatric, and surgical services. An eight-story building located in Bole sub city, in the vicinity of Jacros village, which was built for hospital services, has been leased for 10 years. Renovation is underway, and the import of the necessary equipment and furniture has been completed. The MCH and Surgery Specialty Center is expected to be operational by the first week of September 2015.

Liyana-Addis HC Share Company (Gondar)

This share company is the first regional initiative of LDHCS (with the largest share) and was established in collaboration with Addis Gize Share Company and a group of senior specialists who practice at Gondar Comprehensive Specialized Referral Hospital. This initiative is expected to address the acute shortage of advanced diagnostic and tertiary care services in Gondar, in particular, and in the northern part of Ethiopia, in general. The facility is located in Gondar, Chechella, just in front of Gondar Specialized Referral Hospital. Liyana-Addis Healthcare (Yanet Multispecialty Center-Gondar) will provide advanced medical and surgical services, as well as state-of-the-art imaging services, including high- resolution CT scanning and advanced laboratory services. The facility renovation is nearing completion, some of the required equipment has been delivered, and the rest is being imported.The facility is expected to be operational by mid-August 2015 E.C

Juba Kidney Hospital and Diagnostic Services (South Sudan)

The Juba Kidney Hospital and Diagnostic Services project was initiated in response to an invitation from the Ministry of Health of the Republic of South Sudan, Honorable Minister Yolanda Awel, and with the facilitation of the respective embassies in Addis Ababa and Juba. The project is a public-private partnership (PPP) between LDHCS and the MOH of the Republic of South Sudan. The hospital will provide high-quality kidney care and other related services. The hospital will also provide advanced diagnostic services. The project is expected to benefit a large number of people in South Sudan who are living with kidney disease. The project will also help to improve the quality of healthcare in South Sudan. The project is expected to be launched by October 2016
